Fudgy Brownie Recipe

This is a recipe for making fudgy brownies.

Ingredients

  • Plain Flour 200g
  • Chocolate 180g
  • Butter 120g
  • 3 eggs
  • Caster/Icing sugar 250g
  • Cocoa Powder 40g
  • White Chocolate Pieces 150g

Steps

  1. First melt the chocolate and butter together
  2. Add sugar and eggs in a separate bowl and whisk until it forms a thick texture
  3. Mix the sugar and eggs mixture with the melted chocolate and butter mixture
  4. Slowly add plain flour and cocoa powder to the batter
  5. Fold in white chocolate pieces gently
  6. Bake in the oven at 180c for 35mins
